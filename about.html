About SMS Directory NG

Honest, Nigerian-first reviews of bulk SMS platforms.

SMS Directory NG exists to make it easier for Nigerian businesses, churches, NGOs and developers to choose a bulk SMS provider without guesswork. Instead of copying marketing claims, we look at real pricing, minimum recharge amounts, DND support, APIs and honest product experience.

Every platform on this site has a clear “Our honest review” section so you can quickly see the trade-offs — what each provider is best at, and where you should be careful before loading credits.

This directory is independent. Recommendations are based on public pricing, documentation and real usage, not paid placements. Where affiliate links exist (like EBulkSMS or Multitexter), we mark them clearly and never let them change our verdict.
