Privacy Policy

How SMS Directory NG collects and uses information.

1. What we collect

SMS Directory NG is a content site. We do not ask you to create an account or upload contacts. The only information we may collect is basic analytics data (such as page views, device type and approximate location) to understand which pages are useful.

2. Cookies and analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to measure traffic. These tools may set cookies in your browser. We do not sell this data or use it to build individual profiles; it is used only in aggregate to improve the site.

3. Contact information

If you reach out to us via email or Facebook, we will only use your contact details to reply to your message and keep a simple record of the conversation. We do not share these details with bulk SMS providers or any third parties.

4. Links to other sites

Many pages on this directory link out to external bulk SMS providers. Once you leave this site, their terms and privacy policies apply. We encourage you to review those before creating an account or loading credits.

5. Updates

We may update this privacy policy as the site evolves. When we do, we’ll update the date at the top of this page.
