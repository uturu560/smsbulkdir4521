What is an SMS unit?

When you send bulk sms nigeria messages, most platforms bill you in “units”. One unit usually represents a single SMS of up to 160 characters on GSM networks. Once your message is longer than 160 characters, it can be split into two, three or more “pages” — and each page costs extra units.

This means that even if a provider advertises cheap bulk sms nigeria pricing, your real cost depends on how many units each message burns.

How platforms charge units per message

Some Nigerian providers charge 1 unit per page; others charge more than 1 unit per page. For example, Multitexter charges 4 units per SMS page on all networks. A 2‑page message on that route will cost 8 units, not 2.

On a platform like EBulkSMS with 1 unit per page, that same 2‑page message would cost 2 units. Even if the advertised price per unit is slightly higher, the final bill may be lower.

Real‑world example

Imagine you send a 2‑page campaign (about 300+ characters) to 1,000 contacts:

  • Provider A: 1 unit per page at ₦2.00 per unit → 2 units × 1,000 = 2,000 units → ₦4,000.
  • Provider B: 4 units per page at ₦1.90 per unit → 8 units × 1,000 = 8,000 units → ₦15,200.

Provider B looks cheaper on paper (“₦1.90 per unit, cheap bulk sms nigeria”) but you actually pay almost 4x more because of the units per page billing.

What to look for when choosing a platform

  • Check how many units are billed per SMS page on each network.
  • Check whether long messages are concatenated and how many pages your typical message will use.
  • Compare both the price per unit and the units‑per‑page policy before deciding which is the best bulk sms platform nigeria for you.
